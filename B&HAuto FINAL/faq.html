What happens if my own finance falls through for a car?

Call B&H Auto’s as we offer finance and can guide you through the process with our finance rep.


What if I have a problem with my car after I have just purchased it?

Call B&H Auto’s straight away to avoid any disappointment.


What happens if I have a problem later with my purchased car?

Check our website link www.bhautos.com.au/warranty.html and see if you are still covered by the “Motor Vehicle Dealers Act 1973”.

If you purchased an extended warranty from “Australian Warranty Network” please contact AWN at www.australianwarranty.com.au


What are my rights under the law if I have a problem once I have purchased a used car?

You are protected by the “Motor Vehicle Dealers Act 1973”.

Please see this link www.bhautos.com.au/warranty.html for more information and for our extended warranty options which are available at the time of the original purchase.


Can I trade in my old car?

Please call us first to discuss it and then bring your vehicle to B&H Auto’s, as all possible trade-ins must be inspected and background checked first before any trade-ins of cars could be considered.


Do you buy used cars?

Please call us first to discuss it and then bring your vehicle to B&H Auto’s, as all possible buys must be inspected and background checked first before any buying of cars from us could be considered.
